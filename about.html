The Band

Origins

StarThistle, established in 2025 and based in Redding, California, plays 90’s rock covers as well as hard-hitting originals. We bring the grit, the energy, and the unfiltered sound of an era that changed music forever.

Follow along for gig dates, releases and updates as we carve our path through the Northern California rock scene.
